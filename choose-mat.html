One size never fits all, at least not perfectly. In order to choose the perfect mat for your unique needs, ask yourself the following questions:

  • Do I need to carry my mat to a studio or do I want it just for home practice?
  • Do I travel a lot and need to easily fit my mat into a suitcase/backpack?
  • Do I want my mat to be eco-friendly and biodegradable?
  • Do I need my mat to provide a lot of cushioning (especially if you have sensitive joints and practice mostly slower styles of yoga like Yin and Restorative)?
  • Do I need my mat to provide excellent grip, even when I sweat (very important if you practice more dynamic styles of yoga)?
